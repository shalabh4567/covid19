About COVID-19

COVID-19 is a disease identified as the cause of an outbreak of respiratory first detected in Whuhan, China.


Covid-19 or Coronavirus are the family of viruses which may cause illness in animals or humans. In several humans coronavirus are known to cause respiratory infections ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East respiratory Symdrome(SARS). Read more

Covid-19 or the Novalcoronavirus is the global pandemic.Health officials say that it originated from in a market in Wuhan, China that sold live and dead wild animals that people ate for food, improved health and vitality and a number of other purposes. One of the issues that has contributed to the spread of 2019-nCOV virus is its two week incubation period. During this time, a person may have the virus, display no symptoms of it, but can still spread it to others.

Myths about COVID-19

Covid-19 effects only to old people!

It effects people of all age group but it becomes more powerful in people above age of 60 due to low immunity.

People should not wear mask while exercising!

People should NOT wear masks when exercising as masks may reduce the ability to breathe comfortably. Sweat can make the mask become wet more quickly which makes it difficult to breathe and promotes the growth of microorganisms.

Drinking alcohol protects you from coronaVirus!

No it doest not protect you from coronavirus. The harmful use of alcohol increases the risk of health problem.

Thermal scanners can detect COVID-19!

No, thermal scanners are effective in detecting people who have fever. They cannot detect people who are infected with COVID-19.
